Software

Desktop apps for creators

Free and open-source downloadable software for vector design, 3D modeling, and mesh processing.

Vector & 2D design

For designing templates, stencils, and decals.

  • Inkscape (Free / GPL)
    Professional vector graphics editor. Essential for refining SVG templates and designing decals.
  • GIMP (Free / GPL)
    Raster graphics editor for photo editing and texture creation.
  • Krita (Free / GPL)
    Digital painting tool, great for sketching designs and surface patterns.

3D modeling (CAD)

For designing ribs, stamps, and molds.

  • OpenSCAD (Free / GPL)
    Code-based 3D modeler. The standard for parametric pottery tools (ribs, rollers).
  • FreeCAD (Free / LGPL)
    Parametric CAD for precise mechanical parts, jigs, and mold negatives.
  • Blender (Free / GPL)
    Advanced 3D suite for organic sculpting, rendering, and complex mold making.
  • Torrify (Free / Pro)
    AI-powered parametric design tool. Turns text descriptions into adjustable 3D models for printing.
