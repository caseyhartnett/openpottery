Template tool

Pottery template generator

We are developing a browser-based tool to help you design and print custom patterns for slab-built pottery.

Features in development

  • Parametric cup, bowl, and plate profiles
  • Custom shrinkage compensation
  • PDF and SVG export for hand-cutting or plotters
  • Saved presets for common studio forms

Coming soon

The first version of our generator is currently under construction.
