Printable tools for clay work

A curated collection of open-source tools you can print at home. From texture rollers to trimming aids.

Texture & surface

Rollers and stamps for surface decoration.

  • Texture rollers collection
    Huge set including wicker, basket weave, and geometric patterns.
  • Pottery coil maker
    Extrude consistent coils.
  • Valentine's Day rollers
    Themed surface texture patterns.

Forming & ribs

Essential ribs and shaping tools.

  • Pottery rib tool #031
    Standard profile rib for smoothing and shaping.
  • Pottery rib 109A
    Bubble cup profile for internal shaping.
  • Shrinkage caliper
    Parametric tool to measure wet to fired size.

Slab building

Cutters, guides, and jigs for slab work.

  • Clay slab cutter
    0.5cm spacing for consistent slab preparation.
  • Thickness guides
    Metric infinite thickness guides for rolling slabs.
  • Tapered cup jig
    Practical helper for handbuilding consistent cups.

Wheel & trimming

Helpers for the pottery wheel.

  • Trimming spinner tool
    Uses a standard 608 bearing for smooth trimming.
  • Banding wheel
    Full-size banding wheel with 6202Z bearing.
  • Pottery rim cones
    For slipcasting galleries and refining rims.
