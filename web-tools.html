Web tools

Online generators & calculators

Browser-based tools for designing templates, calculating glazes, and managing your studio. No installation required.

Template generators

Create custom patterns for slab building and packaging.

  • Templatemaker.nl
    Excellent for generating boxes, truncated cones (cups/bowls), and packaging nets.
  • Pottery slab mug template generator
    Specialized for mugs with shrinkage calculation built-in.
  • OpenPottery template tool
    Hosted as a standalone app at tool.openpottery.com.

Glaze & materials

Chemistry calculators and material databases.

  • Glazy.org
    The leading open community database for ceramic recipes and UMF calculation.
  • Digitalfire Insight
    Comprehensive reference for ceramic materials, oxides, and technical troubleshooting.

Kiln & firing

Tools for firing schedules and kiln management.

  • Pi kiln controller (GitHub)
    Web-enabled kiln controller software for Raspberry Pi.
