About

Our mission

OpenPottery is built as a straightforward online hub for free pottery resources, practical tools, and openly shared making knowledge.

Open access for makers

We believe that pottery knowledge and studio tools should be accessible to everyone. By collecting public domain texts, sharing open-source 3D designs, and building free web-based tools, we aim to lower the barrier to entry for both traditional and tech-forward ceramic artists.
