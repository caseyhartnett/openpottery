Featured Creators

A categorized list of standout pottery educators and creators for different learning goals.

Florian Gadsby

The Gold Standard (Cinematic & Precision)

Arguably the most popular potter on the platform right now. His videos are beautifully shot, ASMR-quality, and focus on disciplined, precise wheel throwing with detailed narration.

Hsinchuen Lin

The Gold Standard (Cinematic & Precision)

A master of precision known for chattering texture techniques and incredibly thin porcelain work. His videos are essential for advanced trimming study.

Simon Leach

The Teachers (Technique & Education)

Often considered the grandfather of YouTube pottery. His videos are raw and unedited, but his wheel-throwing and tool-making knowledge is unmatched.

Bill van Gilder

The Teachers (Technique & Education)

An excellent resource for functional ware like mugs, bowls, and casseroles. His workshop-style teaching is strong on the mechanics of why forms function well.

Tim See

The Teachers (Technique & Education)

Known for a non-scary approach to firing and wheel throwing, with complex topics broken into understandable bite-sized lessons.

Pottery to the People

Handbuilding & Home Studio

One of the best channels for handbuilding without a wheel, focused on approachable projects with minimal equipment for home practice.

Little Street Pottery

Handbuilding & Home Studio

Clear, project-based tutorials that blend handbuilding and wheel throwing, often centered on decorative and functional items.

John Britt

Glazing & Science

The definitive source for glaze understanding. Essential for learning glaze chemistry, color outcomes, and mixing your own materials.

Washington Street Studios

Glazing & Science

Excellent deep dives into kiln physics, firing schedules, and clay body behavior.

Old Forge Creations

Modern & Experimental

A UK-based potter known for combining modern technology with ceramics, especially 3D-printed molds and tools for complex geometric forms.

Earth Nation Ceramics

Modern & Experimental

High-energy and entertaining content covering business realities, selling pottery, and experimental firing techniques.

Ingleton Pottery

Production Throwing

If you want to study speed and workflow efficiency, this UK production potter demonstrates high-volume throwing in real time.

Ancient Pottery

Additional Creator

Andy Ward's channel dedicated to ancient pottery making and historical Southwest techniques.

Additional Creator

Curated pottery inspiration and process clips from around the world.
